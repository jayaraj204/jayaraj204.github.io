Power BI Dashboard – Amazing-Mart EU2 Sales Analysis

2024

Project Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance, profit trends, and shipping behavior for Amazing-Mart EU2, a fictional European retail dataset. The dashboard provides regional and city-level breakdowns to identify high-performing markets and optimize shipping strategies. Developed as part of a Power BI learning journey, the project emphasizes visual storytelling and actionable business intelligence.

Objectives

  • Visualize total sales, profit margins, and order volume by country and region.
  • Identify top-performing cities and customer segments.
  • Analyze shipping modes and their usage trends.
  • Enable data-driven decisions through interactive visual components.

Technologies Used

  • Microsoft Power BI
  • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
  • Power Query (M Language)
  • Excel (data source formatting and preparation)

Skills Demonstrated

    • Data Modeling
    • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
    • Data Visualization
    • Reporting & Insights
    • Dashboard Design & Interpretation

Key Highlights

  • Total Sales: €2M across Europe.
  • Top Countries: France, Germany, and the UK lead in order volume.
  • Top Cities: London, Vienna, and Berlin drive the highest order counts.
  • Shipping Analysis: Economy mode accounts for 59% of shipments.
  • Regional Focus: Central Europe contributes 54% of total orders.

Conclusion

This Power BI dashboard demonstrates proficiency in transforming raw data into meaningful insights. With dynamic visuals, slicers, and KPIs, the project showcases a clear understanding of data storytelling, regional sales strategy, and logistics optimization. It is a strong example of my capability in business intelligence and dashboard development.
